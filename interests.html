My Interests & Hobbies

Beyond the world of infrastructure engineering, I engage in a variety of interests and hobbies that help me relax, stay creative, and continuously learn. These activities provide a refreshing balance to my technical pursuits and often contribute to my problem-solving abilities in unexpected ways.

Outdoor Adventures: Hiking & Nature Photography

I find immense joy and mental clarity in exploring natural landscapes. Hiking allows me to disconnect from screens and reconnect with the environment, while nature photography helps me appreciate intricate details and develop a keen eye for composition – a skill transferable to design.

Reading: Sci-Fi & Non-Fiction

My reading habits span from thought-provoking science fiction, which often inspires innovative solutions, to non-fiction books on technology, philosophy, and history. Reading expands my knowledge base and critical thinking.

Personal Coding Projects

I enjoy working on small personal coding projects in my spare time. Whether it's automating a home task with Python or experimenting with new web technologies, these projects keep my coding skills sharp and satisfy my curiosity for how things work beyond my professional scope.

Cooking & Culinary Exploration

Experimenting with new recipes and cuisines is a creative outlet for me. It's akin to engineering – understanding ingredients, processes, and achieving a desired outcome, all while enjoying the delicious results!
